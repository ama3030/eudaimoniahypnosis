Afterwards, he went back to school and finished his degree in Philosophy at Georgia State University. During the 2020 pandemic, he started feeling his depression return along with anxiety and fear. It was then that he decided to see Sean Wheeler, who later became his mentor...

Using hypnosis and NLP, Arno has learned how to control and re-program his own mind to live a life worth living. Now, as a Certified Clinical Hypnotherapist, he is doing the same for others. To help them take control of their lives again- to live their dreams, and to see the beauty and love that exists in the world all around us. Arno truly believes that we deserve to live healthy, happy, and flourishing lives. Stop the unnecessary suffering and start transforming your life today.
